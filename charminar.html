Charminar

Charminar was built by Sultan Mohammed Quli Qutb Shah in 1591. It is said that this square-shaped structure with four pillars and intricate carvings was built in honour of his wife, Bhagmati.

As per popular belief, Charminar was built to acknowledge the elimination of the plague that affected Hyderabad. The four pillars are said to represent the first four caliphs of Islam.
