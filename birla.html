Birla Mandir

Birla Mandir is a beautiful Hindu temple located on a 280-foot high hillock called Naubath Pahad in Hyderabad. It is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara (a form of Vishnu).

Built entirely out of white marble, the temple blends Rajasthani, Dravidian, and Utkala architectural styles. It is one of the major spiritual destinations in the city and offers a breathtaking view of Hyderabad.
