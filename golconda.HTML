Golconda Fort

Golconda Fort is one of the most magnificent fortress complexes in India, located in Hyderabad. Originally a mud fort built by the Kakatiyas, it was later fortified by the Qutb Shahi dynasty.

Known for its amazing acoustics and stunning architecture, Golconda was once famous for the diamond market that produced some of the world's most precious gems, including the Koh-i-Noor.
