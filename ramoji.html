Ramoji Film City

Ramoji Film City, located in Hyderabad, is the largest integrated film city in the world and holds a Guinness World Record. It was founded by Ramoji Rao in 1991.

Spanning over 2,000 acres, it offers film sets, amusement parks, and themed entertainment. It is a major tourist attraction and a popular shooting destination for Indian films.
